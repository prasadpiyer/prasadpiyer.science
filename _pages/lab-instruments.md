## Research Support and Collaboration

Our instrumentation is available for collaborative research projects. For inquiries about instrument access, training, or collaborative opportunities, please [contact us](/contact/).

### Safety and Training

All lab users undergo comprehensive safety training and instrument-specific instruction before gaining access to equipment. We maintain strict safety protocols for laser safety (Class 4 lasers), cryogenic systems, and electrical systems.
