### Overview
We combine **III–V semiconductor resonators** with voltage-triggered insulator–metal transitions in VO₂ to create mid-infrared (MIR) metasurfaces offering >2π phase modulation at sub-10 V bias. Integrated *p*–*n* junctions inject carriers and trigger VO₂ phase changes, providing dual-channel tuning of resonance energy and line width.

### Key contributions
1. **Heterojunction resonator platform** – our *Adv. Optical Mater.* 2016 paper introduced GaInAs/AlInAs resonators experiencing 180° phase shift over 200 nm bandwidth.
2. **Voltage-switched VO₂ devices** – demonstrated 20 dB contrast phase modulators at 5 V in 2023.
3. **MIR beam steering** – 1×64 phased arrays steer 4 µm light by ±30° with 40 kHz update rate.

### Impact
Electrically driven MIR metasurfaces unlock **low-power, solid-state alternatives** to mechanical scanners for spectroscopy, thermal imaging and free-space optical links.
